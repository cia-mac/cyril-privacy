Cyril — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Summary

Cyril does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Your images and videos never leave your Mac.

Data Collection

Cyril collects no data whatsoever. The app processes image files entirely on your local machine. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no network connections.

File Access

Cyril accesses only the folders you explicitly select through the macOS file picker. It reads image files from your chosen source folder and writes rendered video to your chosen destination folder. No other files or folders are accessed.

Network

Cyril makes no network requests. It works entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

Cyril uses no third-party SDKs, frameworks, or services.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
